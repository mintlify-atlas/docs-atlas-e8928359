TTS Voice Wizard is a Windows desktop app designed to give everyone a voice — whether you are non-verbal, have a speech impediment, or simply want a fun way to communicate in VRChat. It works **inside VRChat and outside of it** too, making it useful for streaming, recording, and everyday accessibility.

The app listens to your microphone, transcribes what you say, speaks it aloud through a text-to-speech engine, and simultaneously sends the text to your VRChat avatar via OSC — all in real time.

## Requirements

- **Windows 10 or Windows 11** (64-bit). Older versions of Windows are not officially supported.
- No additional software is required to get started — Windows built-in speech recognition and System Speech TTS are included.

## Free tier and VoiceWizardPro

TTS Voice Wizard is free to use. The free tier includes Windows built-in speech recognition, Whisper local transcription, System Speech TTS, and translation via DeepL (requires your own free DeepL API key).

**VoiceWizardPro** is a premium subscription available on [Ko-Fi](https://ko-fi.com/ttsvoicewizard) or [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/ttsvoicewizard). It unlocks:

- Premium voices from Microsoft Azure, Amazon Polly, Google Cloud, and IBM Watson
- DeepGram Nova-2 speech recognition (fastest and most accurate)
- Translation into 70+ languages
- OpenAI TTS and ChatGPT integration (requires your own OpenAI API key)

See the [VoiceWizardPro overview](/pro/overview) for details on what each tier includes.
