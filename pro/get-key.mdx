---
title: "Getting your VoiceWizardPro key"
description: "Subscribe on Ko-Fi or Patreon and activate your Pro key inside TTS Voice Wizard."
---

After subscribing on Ko-Fi or Patreon, you receive a VoiceWizardPro key that unlocks premium features — cloud voices, Deepgram Nova-2 speech recognition, and managed translation — without needing to set up any third-party API accounts.

## Activate your key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscribe to VoiceWizardPro">
    Choose a subscription tier on [Ko-Fi](https://ko-fi.com/ttsvoicewizard/tiers) or [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/ttsvoicewizard). Both platforms offer the same Pro benefits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your key">
    After subscribing, you'll receive your VoiceWizardPro key via the platform's messaging system or email. Check your Ko-Fi or Patreon inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open TTS Voice Wizard">
    Launch TTS Voice Wizard and navigate to the **VoiceWizardPro** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your key">
    Paste your key into the **VoiceWizardPro Key** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Activate">
    Click **Activate** — premium features unlock immediately. Cloud voice providers and Deepgram Nova-2 will become available in the TTS and STT menus.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Keep your key private — it is tied to your subscription and your monthly usage limits for TTS characters and speech recognition hours.
</Note>

<Tip>
  If you don't receive your key within 24 hours, contact support in the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/YjgR9SWPnW).
</Tip>

<Info>
  Your own API keys for Azure, Google Cloud, Amazon Polly, or IBM Watson still work in the free version — VoiceWizardPro removes the need to manage your own keys by routing requests through TTS Voice Wizard's backend.
</Info>
