TTS Voice Wizard is a free Windows desktop app that gives you a voice in VRChat and beyond. Whether you're mute, have a speech disability, or simply want a custom TTS voice, TTS Voice Wizard bridges the gap — sending your words as live text to your avatar's chatbox or a floating speech bubble.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download TTS Voice Wizard">
    Grab the latest release from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/VRCWizard/TTS-Voice-Wizard/releases) and extract the ZIP file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your speech recognition method">
    Select a speech recognition engine in the app — Windows built-in recognition requires no API key and works immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a TTS voice">
    Choose a text-to-speech engine and voice. System Speech is free and built in; VoiceWizardPro unlocks hundreds of premium cloud voices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to VRChat">
    Enable OSC in VRChat (**Action Menu → Options → OSC → Enable**), then start speaking — your words will appear on your avatar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  TTS Voice Wizard works outside VRChat too — use it for OBS streaming text, Discord calls, or any situation where you need a voice.
</Note>
